The Name

If you are redesigning your website then you already have a domain, you should continue to the next step.

The domain name is one of the most important aspects of your website. People hate typing in long domain names, so having a short name is important. Also, people are familiar with the .com domain ending so you should try to stick to that whenever possible. There are new domain endings being added all the time that cary some novelty or uniqueness, such as .website, .attorney, or .club.

When choosing a domain name using your business or personal name is usually the easiest route. Unfortunately, many common names are already taken. This just means we have to get creative. Using the box below you can search for available domain names instantly and even receive some suggestions.


Use the text box below to enter 2 or 3 short keywords that describe your business and we can suggest some names that are available and might work.
