Introduction

We built this guide because of a problem that we noticed among our clients. Many business owners know they need a website, or even a redesign of their current one, but they do not know where to go and who to trust to get it. They are perfectly willing to do it themselves, but can't spend days researching how. We provide you with an exact step by step blueprint to follow. All the unimportant decisions have been made for you. Where to host, where to get a domain, what platform to use. All you have to do is choose a domain name and fill in your content.

We tried to make this guide as easy as possible to follow for three reasons: First, your job is to run a business, not learn to code (unless your business is coding, in which case you probably shouldn't be here). Second, making the guide as easy as possible means that you should never get stuck and need help from someone else. Third, an easy guide is a quick guide. Our goal is to get you from start to finish in one weekend. If you already have a domain or hosting good for you, that just means you can finish that much faster.

This guide is completely free. This guide can be free because of the affiliate links that we use throughout the guide. Please use these affiliate links when you can, it helps keep this site up and running and this guide free.
